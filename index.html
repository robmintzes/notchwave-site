NotchWave

Transform your MacBook notch into a real-time audio visualizer

NotchWave captures your system audio and renders dynamic pixel-grid visualizations directly in your MacBook's notch. Powered by CoreAudio and Metal for buttery-smooth, real-time FFT analysis.

  • Real-time frequency band visualization
  • Multiple visual modes — sparks, patterns, and more
  • Adjustable sensitivity and color schemes
  • Lightweight menu-bar app — runs unobtrusively
